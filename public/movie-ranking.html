My Favorite Movies

Top 3 movies of all time.

Weird Science

Fantastic movie about overcoming adversity and how to recognize and value what is truly important in life. It also has a story about a girl with big big titties anda telephone.

Ninja Scroll

First anime movie I watched as an adult and I love it. Story about Jubei, Kagero and the 12 demons. Great fights, awesome visuals and an insane final fight.

Die Hard

Classic action flick with Bruce Willis and Alan Rickman. Movie doe snot try to be anythign but a wild rollercoaster ride.
